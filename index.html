📜 Privacy Policy – Bseba App

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Bseba App is a multi-service platform that provides:

  • Doctor appointment booking
  • Blood donor & request matching
  • House rental listings
  • Part-time job postings
  • Home service connections
  • Health checker tools
  • Reward & cashback system
  • User support and messaging

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we collect and use your information.

2. Information We Collect

A. Personal Information

  • Full Name
  • Date of Birth / Age
  • Gender
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address
  • Division / District / Area / Thana
  • Profile Photo (optional)

B. Health-Related Information

  • Blood Group
  • Doctor appointment details
  • Symptoms selected in Health Checker
  • Medical service requests

⚠️ Important: Bseba App does NOT provide medical diagnosis or treatment. Health data is used only for service facilitation.

C. User Generated Content

  • Blood donation requests
  • Rental listings
  • Job postings
  • Home service requests
  • Uploaded receipts
  • Chat messages
  • Ratings & reviews

D. Financial / Reward Data

  • Medical expense receipts
  • Reward point history
  • Cashback claims

E. App Activity

  • Booking requests
  • Service interactions
  • Notification preferences
  • Login activity

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Facilitate doctor bookings
  • Connect blood donors & recipients
  • Publish rental and job listings
  • Match users with service providers
  • Process reward and cashback requests
  • Verify uploaded receipts
  • Improve app performance
  • Provide customer support
  • Send important service notifications

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Sharing

  • Between service requester and provider (after acceptance)
  • Between blood donor and recipient
  • Between job poster and applicant
  • With internal admins for verification
  • When required by law

We do not share your data for advertising resale purposes.

5. Data Storage & Security

We implement reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your data. However, no internet-based system is 100% secure. By using Bseba App, you acknowledge this risk.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary for:

  • Providing app services
  • Legal compliance
  • Dispute resolution

Users may request account deletion at any time.

7. User Rights & Control

  • Update profile information
  • Delete posts or uploaded content
  • Request account deletion
  • Contact support for data-related concerns

To request deletion, email: support@bseba.com

8. Children’s Privacy

Bseba App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If such data is identified, it will be removed promptly.

9. Third-Party Services

Bseba App may use third-party services such as Firebase (Authentication, Database, Storage), Hosting providers, and Analytics services. These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

10. Advertising (AdMob)

Bseba App may display advertisements through third-party advertising networks such as Google AdMob. These services may collect:

  • Device information
  • Advertising ID
  • IP address
  • App interaction data

This data may be used to provide personalized or non-personalized ads. Users can control ad personalization through their device settings.

11. Payments & Transactions (Future Use)

If payment features are introduced in the future:

  • Transactions will be processed through secure third-party payment gateways.
  • Bseba App will not store full credit/debit card information on its own servers.
  • Payment data will be handled according to the payment provider’s privacy policy.

12. Analytics

We may use analytics services (such as Firebase Analytics) to understand app usage, improve features, and fix technical issues. Analytics data may include:

  • Device type
  • App version
  • Usage behavior
  • Crash reports

This data does not personally identify users.

13. Account Deletion & Data Removal

Users may request:

  • Permanent account deletion
  • Removal of specific uploaded content
  • Data export request

Deletion requests can be sent to: support@bseba.com. We will process valid requests within a reasonable timeframe.

14. Compliance With Google Play Policies

  • Google Play User Data Policy
  • Health Data Policy
  • Families & Children Policy (if applicable)

We only collect data necessary to provide app services.

15. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

16. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact: support@bseba.com
